Frequently Asked Questions

Questions Answers
What Is Penny Juice? Penny Juice is a company that makes concentrated fruit juice specifically for childcare centers and preschools. Their juice is easy to mix, doesn’t require refrigeration, comes in fun flavors (including color-free options), and meets USDA and FDA guidelines.
How Much Does A Penny Juice Cost? Penny Juice doesn’t actually cost a penny— Prices vary by case, but typically a case (which makes up to 48 gallons) costs around $60–$70. Still a sweet deal for a whole lot of juice!
What Flavors Do You Offer? Penny Juice offers 15 kid-friendly flavors, including: Apple, White Grape, Strawberry Banana, Strawberry Kiwi, Cherry, Orange, Grape, Fruit Punch, Pineapple, Tropical Punch, Kiwi Strawberry, Blue Raspberry, Peach Mango, Lemonade, Watermelon, etc. They also have 7 color-free options—same fruity taste, just without the added colors. Perfect for picky parents and happy kids! Check out the Shop Flavors Page!
Is Penny Juice Really a Penny? Penny Juice might sound like it costs just a penny, but nope—unless you’ve got a time machine and a really generous fruit vendor!
Is Penny Juice Healthy? Yes, Penny Juice is considered a healthier option for kids—it’s made from 100% fruit juice concentrate, contains no added sugar, and meets USDA and FDA nutritional guidelines.
Is Penny Juice Vegan? Penny Juice offers 100% blended fruit juice concentrates specifically designed for children in childcare settings. Their products are sugar-free, made from concentrated fruit juice, and meet USDA and FDA nutritional guidelines. The ingredients include concentrated fruit juice, filtered water, malic acid, and ascorbic acid (vitamin C). While these ingredients are plant-based and typically considered vegan, Penny Juice does not explicitly label their products as vegan. Therefore, if strict vegan certification is important to you, it's advisable to contact Penny Juice directly for confirmation.
